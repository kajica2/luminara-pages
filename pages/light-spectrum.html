The Science

Light affects biology. This isn't woo.

Photobiomodulation, circadian science, and chromotherapy research inform every aspect of Light Spectrum. We bridge peer-reviewed science with wisdom traditions.

Photobiomodulation

Specific wavelengths of light stimulate mitochondrial function and cellular repair. Red and near-infrared light are particularly well-studied for tissue healing.

Circadian Regulation

Blue light (460-480nm) suppresses melatonin. Warm light (>580nm) supports natural sleep cycles. Light Spectrum uses this to optimize your daily rhythm.

Color Psychology

Decades of research confirm color's effect on mood, cognition, and physiological arousal. Light Spectrum translates this into personalized therapeutic sessions.

Vibrational Medicine

Ancient systems — Ayurveda, TCM, Tibetan medicine — associate specific colors with organs and energy centers. We honor this lineage while grounding it in modern data.
